Mackenzie and Laura graduated from Holy Names Academy in 2016. Both are studying Computer Science: Mackenzie at Villanova and Laura at the University of Washington.

Their inspiration for starting WATT is rooted in the mentorship that they received as young women, and the belief that continuing the cycle of mentorship is an opportunity to grow personally and to uplift the computer science field for women.



Our Core Team


Madeleine Doi ~ Core Team Leader

I'm a currently senior at Holy Names Academy. I was first introduced to computer science in 8th grade when participated in a Computer Science Camp. Through the camp I discovered how to print my name to the terminal window. I also uncovered various solutions to improve my life through computer science such as developing and implementing my own calculators and algorithms. I quickly grew my interest in and passion for computer science. Volunteering at WATT enables me to inspire other young women to explore computer science.
