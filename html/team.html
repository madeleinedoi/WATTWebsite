WATT is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 by two Holy Names graduates as a way to introduce girls to computer science at a young age. We are dedicated to bringing computer science to middle school aged girls throughout the Greater Seattle Area. Our organization is run by a core team of juniors and seniors.

Founders


Mackenzie
Jorgensen

Laura
DeBoldt


Mackenzie and Laura graduated from Holy Names Academy in 2016. Both are studying Computer Science: Mackenzie at Villanova and Laura at the University of Washington.

Their inspiration for starting WATT is rooted in the mentorship that they received as young women, and the belief that continuing the cycle of mentorship is an opportunity to grow personally and to uplift the computer science field for women.
