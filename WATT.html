Our Mission


WATT is a non-profit dedicated to introducing girls to computer science at a young age. We aim to create a safe environment for learning and exploring at K-8 schools in the greater Seattle area. We provide strong female role models in the form of our student volunteers with a focus on girls empowering girls. seasonal ingredients.

About Us

WATT is a non-profit organization founded in 2015 by two Holy Names graduates as a way to introduce girls to computer science at a young age. We are dedicated to bringing computer science to middle school aged girls throughout the Greater Seattle Area. Our organization is run by a core team of juniors and seniors.
